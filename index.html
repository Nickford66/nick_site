Why Federated Learning?

  • Privacy by design — raw data never leaves your device.
  • Diverse learning — gains insight from global experience.
  • Hyper-personal — models adapt to local context & culture.
  • Resilient network — no single point of failure or control.
  • Reg-ready — native compliance with GDPR, HIPAA & AI acts.

Architecture Snapshot

  • Local Fine-Tuning + Redis RAG — on-device LoRA & vector DB.
  • P2P Mesh — encrypted weight gossip between peers.
  • Hybrid Validation — device tests + steward Benchmarks.
  • Blockchain Anchoring — immutable audit trail of updates.
  • Flower Foundation — trusted federated-learning layer.
